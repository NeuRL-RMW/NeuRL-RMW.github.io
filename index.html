Overview

The field of robotics has made significant advancements in recent years, thanks to the integration of machine learning techniques. In particular, neural representation learning has emerged as a powerful tool for enabling robots to perceive and manipulate their environments effectively. This workshop aims to explore the intersection of neural representation learning and robot manipulation, providing participants with a comprehensive understanding of the latest developments, challenges, and opportunities in this exciting area of research.
